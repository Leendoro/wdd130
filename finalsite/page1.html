Protagonists

Here we are introduced to our band of "heros" while dubious and unorhtidox these guys are your playable party memebers

Trashboy

The main character is Trashboy, hes a bit of a blank slate and not very vocal, in the game youll be playing as him and youll learn more of his story, hes a street orphan, taken in buy his teacher who is a skilled theif and burgaler

The Brain Surgeon

The next character you meet is The Brain Surgeon, he'll act as the party healer, hes a bit scary, but he really has a good heart! trust me!

The Mechanic

The Mechanic is another friend youll maake along the way, shes been injured from a past raid by one of the various warlords, but her skills with machies are unmached in the wasteland of the old world

The Diesle

Diesle is the forth party memeber you'll meet, hes a strange guy really, freakishly strong and unmatched at the wheel, while a bit ecentric he'll surely pull his weight!

The Clown

The Clown is a secret partymember you can befriend in the game, throughout the map youll find im in some sort of problem, when you help him enough times he'll join up with you! hes funny and caring and overall a joy to be around, it seems his past employer though wont be to happy with his departure...

The Tissue Vender

The Tissue vender is the first major boss youll fight in the quest to find your teacher, however showing your cleanliness to this clean freak will have him join your party, hes pretty sketchy, obsessed with cleanliness he runs the clean district, sending out cleaners to clean up messes for contractors, take that how you will.

Lanceolot

Lanceolot is a strange creature called a milk, he acts like an old heroic knight, waving his sword in defence of his friends, although he dosnt really have any, the rest of his vilage ostrisize him, so after helping him out he pledges his sword for your aid!

As shown before here is everyone together, this band of unlikely friends brave the unkown wasteland to discover its horrors, mysteries and secrets!
