DEADWORLD Devsite

Deadworld is a game I've been developing for about a year, firstly its still in its infant stages, but im exited to share what ive got so far Dead world is a Post apocalyptic RPG set place in an unmeasurable amount of time in the future after the world was nuked to oblivian humanity and other forms of life still live and it seems society is making a re-emergence but things still really suck all the time, warlords rule the land for territory and power, monstrosities roam the land, and secrets and issues from the old world rear their ugly heads in this new age
